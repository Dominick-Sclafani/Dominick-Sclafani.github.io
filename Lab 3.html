Why Neuromancer is my favorite book of all time.

Neuromancer was released in 1984 by William Gibson in a time in which society had just invented the Internet at its very young point.

  • Released as a Sci-fi novel before Cyberpunk was termed at a time when the Internet was very new and all these concepts were new.
  • Coined the term Cyberspace in the book to what we know like in the Matrix etc.
  • Gave a dystopian near-future that serves as a warning to way things can go with technology and Artifical Intelligence but keeps it fiction enough to be fun!
